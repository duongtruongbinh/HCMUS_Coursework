1.1. Bài toán tối ưu lồi ¶

kho st tnh li lm ca hm s nhiu bin, ta xt o hm ring cp 1 v o hm ring cp 2 ca $f$.

V d 1. Xt tnh li/lm ca hm s sau $$f(x_1,x_2,x_3)=x_1^2+x_2^2+x_3^2-x_1x_2+x_1x_3$$

Ta c ma trn xc nh dng $A$ ca hm $f$ nh sau $$A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -1/2 & 1/2\\ -1/2 & 1 & 0\\ 1/2 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$$ kim tra tnh li/lm ca $f$, ta tm cc tr ring ng vi ma trn $A$.

1.2. Bài toán bình phương tối tiểu (Least square) ¶

Xt ma trn A c kch thc m x n (m > n) v vector b c kch thc m. Bnh phng ti tiu $\hat{r}$ ca A v b trn gi tr $x$ c tnh nh sau:

$$\hat{r} = ||Ax - b||^{2}$$ $$\hat{x} = (A^TA)^{-1}A^Tb$$

V d Cho ma trn $A$ v vector ct $b$ nh sau \begin{align*} A=\begin{pmatrix} 2&0\\ -1&1\\ 0&2 \end{pmatrix}, b=\begin{pmatrix} 1\\0\\-1 \end{pmatrix} \end{align*} Tm nghim ca bi ton bnh phng ti tiu $$||Ax - b||^{2}$$

1.3. Mô hình hồi quy tuyến tính (Linear regression model) ¶

Nghim $\hat{x}$ ca phng trnh hi quy tuyn tnh Ax = b c tnh nh sau: $$\hat{x} = (A^{T}A)^{-1}A^{T}b = A^{+}b$$

Kho st 2 i lng X,Y . Cho bng d liu nh sau:

X Y
1 2
2 2
3 5
4 8

Vi mi m hnh c cho, xc nh cc tham s ca m hnh v tnh chun vector phn d.

In [8]:
xs = [1, 2, 3, 4]
ys = [2, 2, 5, 8]
print(xs, ys)

[1, 2, 3, 4] [2, 2, 5, 8]

thun tin xy dng m hnh, hm getAb(xs,ys) c vit.

Input¶

  • xs, ys ln lt l vector d liu ca 2 bin X,Y ban u.

Output¶

  • hm tr v ma trn d liu A b sung thm ct s 1 v ma trn ct b dng tm cc h s cho m hnh hi quy.

def getAb(xs, ys):
    col1 = np.ones(len(xs))
    colx = np.array(xs)
    A = np.array([col1, colx]).T
    b = np.array(ys).reshape(len(ys), 1)
    return A, b
