1. Suppose that Starbucks wanted to conduct a study to determine whether men and women differ in the amount of money they spend when they visit one of their stores.
they hired a researcher to collect these data from the first 100 customers to 1 of their stores in New York 1 day.
40 men, 60 women
1. What kind of sampling method did the researcher use?
convenience sampling

lượng tiền sử dụng của 100 khách hàng khi ghé cửa hàng
3. what is/are the population(s) in this study?
Toàn bộ khách hàng của starbucks ở new york
4. Do you think the results of this study will generalize to the population(s)?
Why or why not?
Không thể generalize cho population vì không chắc sẽ lấy được hết tất cả đặc trưng của population (độ tuổi, nghề nghiệp, tầng lớp,chi nhánh, thời gian ...)
5. what are the independent and dependent variables in this study?
independent: gender
dependent: money
6. what type of measurement does each of these variables use? (nominal, ordinal, interval, ratio)
nominal: sex
continous: money
7. what kind of study is this (experimental, quasi-experimental, correlational)?
Correlational research designs

statistics: 100 customer, 40 nam , 40 nữ
